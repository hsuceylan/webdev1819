Cheesecake

  • 401 : Calories
  • 28 g : Total fat
  • 32 g : Carbohydrates
  • Allergy Warning


Ingredients

  • Crust

  • 1 and 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 5 Tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

  • Cheesecake

  • Four 8-ounce blocks full-fat cream cheese
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup full-fat sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3 large eggs

  • Optional Toppings

  • Salted caramel
  • Lemon curd
  • Fresh fruit
  • Chocolate ganache
  • Whipped cream
  • Raspberry sauce
