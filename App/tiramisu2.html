Tiramisu

Instructions

  • Stir espresso powder into hot water.
  • Dip each cookie completely in the espresso, submerging it and allowing it to sit for a couple of seconds. Arrange in an 8x8" baking dish. Set aside.
  • Beat cream cheese with a hand mixer until smooth, then mix in powdered sugar and both extracts. Beat until smooth.
  • Beat heavy whipping cream until stiff peaks form (using a hand or a stand mixer).
  • Fold whipped cream into cream cheese mixture.
  • Spread cream cheese mixture over the top of the shortbread cookies in the pan.
  • Finely grate chocolate over the top evenly or dust with cocoa powder.
  • Chill at least 2 hours before serving but up to 8 hours. Store covered in refrigerator for up to 2 days.
